## ⚙️ Features

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%">
      <h3>🤖 Multi-Provider AI Engine</h3>
      Dynamically integrates with industry-leading LLMs including <b>OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Groq</b>. Choose the perfect model for your needs directly from the settings panel.
    </td>
    <td width="50%">
      <h3>✨ Premium Glassmorphism UI</h3>
      Features a mesmerizing aesthetic with frosted-glass panels, hardware-accelerated ambient background gradients, and smooth transition animations resulting in a breathtaking aesthetic.
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>
      <h3>🎛️ Detailed Control Parameters</h3>
      Fine-tune the output by selecting custom lengths (Short, Medium, Detailed) and distinct AI styles/tones (Neutral, Formatted, Bullet Points, Simple, etc.).
    </td>
    <td>
      <h3>💾 Built-In Persistence & Exporters</h3>
      Automatically saves your text context and preferences locally via <code>localStorage</code>. Export your final summaries instantly as <code>.txt</code> files or copy seamlessly to clipboard.
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>

## 🔐 Privacy First

> **Your Keys, Your Machine.**
> The AI Text Summarizer ensures your data stays private. All API keys and configuration preferences are securely stored client-side in your own browser's Local Storage. We do not operate a backend server, meaning your prompt data interacts directly with your chosen AI provider natively.
